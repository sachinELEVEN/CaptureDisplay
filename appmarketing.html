CaptureDisplay - Screen Augmentation & Note-Taking for Online Meetings

CaptureDisplay is a powerful macOS application designed to elevate your screen-sharing experience during online meetings (such as Google Meet and Zoom). With intuitive controls, real-time screen manipulation, and instant note-taking capabilities, this app lets you effortlessly manage what you display on your screen and capture key moments. CaptureDisplay makes it simple to organize and save important notes and screenshots directly into a PDF file with just a few shortcuts.

Key Features

Zoom Mode

  • Double Click: Enable or disable zoom mode to focus on specific parts of your screen.
  • Zoom In / Zoom Out: Adjust zoom levels dynamically within zoom mode using custom shortcuts.

Screen Section Control

  • Top-Left & Bottom-Right Points: Define and display specific sections of your screen.
  • Show Entire Screen: Instantly revert to showing the full screen with one click.

Region of Interest Visibility

  • Toggle Blur/Blackout: Switch between blurring or blacking out private screen sections to protect sensitive information.

Effortless Note-Taking

  • Save Copied Text: Automatically save copied content to your notes PDF for quick reference.
  • Take Screenshot: Capture important screen sections and save them directly to the notes file.

Modes & App Controls

  • Pen Mode: Enable/disable pen mode to annotate the screen. Use Option + Trackpad for a smooth drawing experience.
  • Output Screen: Toggle an output screen, specifically designed for taking notes or sharing content as screenshots and copied text.
  • Sleep Mode: Quickly put the app to sleep or wake it up without quitting.
  • Quit App: Exit the app entirely when your meeting or session ends.

CaptureDisplay streamlines note-taking, screen manipulation, and private information management in one seamless experience, making it the perfect tool for professionals who frequently present or share their screens. With an easy-to-use shortcut system, CaptureDisplay ensures that you can focus on what matters during your meetings while keeping critical information organized and secure.
